Meet the Team

Brandon

Brandon is the store owner of Black Diamond sporting goods store. Growing up, Brandon trained in martial arts. He also played football and basketball. His passion for fitness and sports led to his decision to open up his own store.

Anita

Anita grew up in a family of athletes. Her two older brothers who played soccer inspired her to play soccer at an early age. In high school, Anita would find her passion for volleyball and tennis. Anita was a CIF scholar athlete and champion all four years.

Michael

Michael grew up with a passion for outdoor exploration. He grew up in the boy scouts where he learned survival skills. Michael currently spends his free time kayaking with friends on the lake Charlotte.

Kate

Kate is the co-owner of the store. She grew up playing basketball and was CIF scholar athlete for four years. Kate manages the finances and purchasing for the store. She spends her free time hiking.
